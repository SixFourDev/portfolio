About Me

My name is Shane Browning, and I am 28 years old and live in San Diego, CA. I am attending the UCSD Extended Studies Coding Boot Camp, stepping into this new journey of web development and coding. I have a lot of knowledge in crypto and blockchain technology which inspired me to take this step, and hoping to be able to work in the web3 space, and build some highly developed applications in the future! I am a motivated and driven individual who loves problem solving, and loves working with groups to create something incredible. I am hardworking and dedicated, and also a perfectionist to make sure everything works perfectly.

Crypto Experience

I started getting interested in the crypto world around 2017 when one of my good friends told me about Bitcoin and XRP. I fell in love with the tech, and have been researching and trading ever since. Fast forward to 2021, my friends and I created our own project on the Solana Blockchain called "Solana Hills". We developed this runner game with our assets that we've created, and that's what really got me to dive deeper and want to learn how to code and become a developer so I could build these really cool applications myself in the future. I also pictured two of my favorite NFTs I hold from "The Degenerate Ape Academy". Overall, I have a passion in web3, and want to create and build useful tools and applications in the community.
